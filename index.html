MONICA

Morphometrics from Optic Nerve Imaging Contour Analysis

Automated morphometric analysis for PPD-stained optic nerve cross-sections. Upload an image and receive axon counts, cross-sectional area, density measurements, and parenchymal quantification in under a minute.

What MONICA measures

Axon Count

Automated detection and counting of individual axons across the entire nerve cross-section.

Cross-sectional Area

Whole optic nerve boundary extraction for accurate area measurement.

Axon Density

Axons per square millimeter, calculated from count and area.

Parenchymal Fraction

Quantification of non-axonal tissue area within the nerve boundary.
