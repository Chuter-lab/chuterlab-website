MONICA

Morphometrics from Optic Nerve Imaging Contour Analysis

Output metrics

Contour-derived

Axon Count, ON Area (µm², mm²), Axon Density (/mm²), Parenchymal Area, Parenchymal Fraction, Axon+Myelin Fraction

Axon shape

Mean, Std, Median, Min, Max for: Diameter, Perimeter, Circularity, Eccentricity, Solidity

Fiber (axon + myelin)

Mean, Std, Median, Min, Max for: Fiber Area, Fiber Diameter

Myelin

Mean, Std, Median, Min, Max for: Myelin Area, Myelin Thickness, G-Ratio

Totals

Total Axon Area, Total Myelin Area, Total Fiber Area
